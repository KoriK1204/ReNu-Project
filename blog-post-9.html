MacBook Air M2 in 2026: Still Worth Buying?

ReNu Tech Team January 10, 2026 5 min read

Home Blog MacBook Air M2 in 2026: Still Worth Buying?

Even with the M4 chip now available, the M2 MacBook Air remains one of the best laptops you can buy — and at refurbished prices, it's arguably the single greatest value in the entire laptop market. Here's why it still deserves your attention in 2026.

The M2 Chip: Still More Than Enough

Apple's M2 chip was a generational leap when it launched, and two years on it remains one of the fastest chips in any thin-and-light laptop. For web browsing, document editing, video calls, photo editing, coding, and even light video work, the M2 handles everything without breaking a sweat. The performance gap between M2 and M4 is real — but for most users, it's entirely academic.

The Display

The M2 MacBook Air features a 13.6-inch Liquid Retina display with 500 nits of brightness and support for a billion colours. It's a genuinely beautiful screen — sharp, colour-accurate, and comfortable for long working sessions. The notch houses a 1080p webcam, which is a significant upgrade over previous MacBook cameras.

The M2 MacBook Air's design — thinner, lighter, and more refined than the M1 — is still one of the most beautiful laptops ever made. It hasn't aged a day.

Battery Life

Up to 18 hours of real-world battery life. That's the headline — and it lives up to it. Whether you're in meetings all day, working from a café, or travelling, the M2 Air will comfortably outlast your workday. It's rare to even think about a charger with this machine.

MagSafe & Ports

The M2 MacBook Air brought back MagSafe charging, freeing up both USB-C ports for peripherals and accessories. You also get a headphone jack that supports high-impedance headphones — a nice touch for audiophiles. It's not the most port-rich laptop, but for most users it's plenty.

Software Support Longevity

Apple typically supports Macs with software updates for 7–8 years. The M2 MacBook Air launched in 2022, which means you're looking at updates through at least 2029–2030. That's exceptional longevity for a refurbished purchase — your investment is well protected.

Refurbished Price: The Killer Argument

A brand new M4 MacBook Air starts at $1,099. A Grade A refurbished M2 MacBook Air with 8GB RAM and 256GB storage starts at just $649 at ReNu Tech — a saving of $450. For a laptop that will handle everything most people throw at it for the next four to five years, that's an extraordinary deal.

Our Verdict

The M2 MacBook Air in 2026 is still a genuinely excellent laptop — and at refurbished prices, there's almost nothing else in the market that touches it for value. If you don't need cutting-edge AI features or the last 20% of M4 performance, save the $450 and spend it on something else. This machine will serve you brilliantly.
