Top 5 Refurbished iPhones of 2026

ReNu Tech Team April 5, 2026 5 min read

Home Blog Top 5 Refurbished iPhones of 2026

Looking for a great deal on an iPhone without compromising on quality? Refurbished iPhones are an excellent choice — and at ReNu Tech, every device goes through a rigorous quality check before it reaches your hands. Here are our top 5 picks for 2026.

1. iPhone 16 Pro — Best Overall

The iPhone 16 Pro is the crown jewel of our refurbished lineup. With Apple's A18 Pro chip, a stunning 6.3-inch Super Retina XDR display, and an industry-leading camera system, this phone punches as hard as a brand new flagship — at a fraction of the price.

Our refurbished units are graded A or A+, meaning the device looks and performs like new, with a fresh battery and a 12-month warranty included.

2. iPhone 15 Pro — Best for Photography

If camera performance is your top priority, the iPhone 15 Pro is hard to beat. The 48MP main sensor, ProRes video recording, and titanium frame make this a powerhouse for creators. Available from $549 refurbished.

Buying refurbished doesn't just save you money — it keeps perfectly functional devices out of landfills. Every purchase at ReNu Tech is a step toward a more sustainable future.

3. iPhone 14 — Best Value

For everyday users who want a solid, reliable iPhone without breaking the bank, the iPhone 14 is the sweet spot. Excellent battery life, a great dual-camera setup, and iOS 18 compatibility make it a smart long-term investment from just $349.

4. iPhone 13 Pro Max — Best Battery Life

Still one of the longest-lasting iPhones ever made. The 13 Pro Max's battery will easily get you through a full day of heavy use. A great pick for road warriors and travelers at around $299.

5. iPhone SE (3rd Gen) — Best Budget Pick

Don't let the compact size fool you — the iPhone SE packs Apple's A15 Bionic chip in the most affordable iPhone form factor. Perfect for first-time iPhone buyers or those who prefer a smaller phone. Starting at just $199 refurbished.

Why Buy Refurbished from ReNu Tech?

Every device we sell has been professionally inspected, tested, and restored to full working condition. We include a 12-month warranty on all refurbished iPhones and offer free returns within 30 days — so you can shop with complete confidence.
