Best Refurbished MacBooks Under $800

ReNu Tech Team February 18, 2026 6 min read

Home Blog Best Refurbished MacBooks Under $800

You don't need to spend over a grand to get a powerful, reliable Mac. The refurbished MacBook market is full of exceptional options at the $800 mark — and at ReNu Tech, we've handpicked the best. Here are our top picks.

1. MacBook Air M2 (8GB / 256GB) — From $649

The M2 MacBook Air is the crown jewel of the sub-$800 refurbished market. With Apple's M2 chip, an 18-hour battery, a stunning Liquid Retina display, and a fanless design that stays completely silent, this is one of the best laptops ever made — at any price. Our Grade A refurbished units look and perform like new, from just $649.

2. MacBook Air M1 (8GB / 256GB) — From $499

Still one of the greatest value laptops on the market. The M1 chip revolutionised what a thin-and-light laptop could do — incredible performance, all-day battery, and zero fan noise. If your budget is tighter, this is the pick. Available from $499 in Grade A condition.

For students, remote workers, and everyday users, the M1 MacBook Air does everything you'll ever need — and does it faster and quieter than most brand-new Windows laptops costing twice as much.

3. MacBook Pro 13" M2 (8GB / 256GB) — From $749

If you need the extra grunt of a Pro model with active cooling, the 13-inch MacBook Pro M2 is a fantastic option under $800. It handles sustained workloads — long video exports, large Xcode projects, intensive multitasking — better than the fanless Air. Available refurbished from $749.

4. MacBook Pro 13" M1 (8GB / 512GB) — From $699

More storage than the base M2 Air, with the performance benefits of the Pro's cooling system. A great pick for developers who need a bit more breathing room on their drive. From $699 at ReNu Tech.

What to Look for When Buying

When choosing a refurbished MacBook, prioritise RAM over storage — 8GB is fine for most users, but 16GB future-proofs you significantly. Storage can always be managed with cloud services or external drives. Also check the battery cycle count — at ReNu Tech, any battery over 500 cycles is replaced before sale.

Our Recommendation

For most people, the refurbished MacBook Air M2 at $649 is the best all-round laptop available at any price point. But every option on this list will serve you brilliantly for years to come.
